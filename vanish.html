Vanish Messenger

Communicate digitally with the same level of security and privacy as a face-to-face conversation.

Watch Overview   52 Seconds

  • Key Management

    Your encryption keys are never uploaded to our server. They are only ever stored on your device.

  • Privacy

    We allow you to stay private by allowing you to use arbitrary username's when signing up. No more signing up with your phone number and exposing your identity.

  • Encryption

    We use only open-source audited cryptography libraries within Vanish to ensure your data stays secure and private. All our encryption uses 256 to 512 bit ciphers.

End-to-End Encryption

Vanish end-to-end encrypts all your communication including messages, photos calls, group calls, and even status times. Only the intended recipient, and nobody else can read your messages.


Message Retraction

Vanish allows you to send messages and erase them of the users device indefinitely, making them as private as the spoken word.


Group Audio & Video Calls

Vanish makes secure communication easy and fun. With audio calls with up to 20,000 participants and video calls with up to 7.

Blockchain Coming Soon

Introducing Blockstore powered by the peer to peer protocol IPFS, each file is stored using patent pending encryption in a decentralised manner. The hash of the file is subsequently stored on the blockchain. Allowing for “storing”, encrypting, and sharing large data and files on the blockchain. Coming to Vanish Messenger soon to allow for added data security and protection.
